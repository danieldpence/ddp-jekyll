About

My name is Daniel Pence, and I'm a web developer from Nashville, TN. I'm curenty seeking opportunities to build awesome things with awesome people. I have a background in entrepreneurship and finance, but my passion is building web applications. While my skillset is mostly geared towards the front-end, I'm certainly not afraid to get my hands dirty on the back end.

I also earned an Associate of Science in Records Arts from Full Sail University in 2004. As the saying goes, "If at first you do not succeed, try, try again."
